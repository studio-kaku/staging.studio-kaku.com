What we do

Services

01

Framing, Installation
& Art Care

A great frame does more than protect — it activates a work. We source, design and produce bespoke frames in dialogue with the art itself, working with conservation-grade materials and trusted specialist craftspeople.

Our installation service covers everything from a single piece in a private home to large-scale hanging programmes for galleries and institutional collections.

We also advise on the long-term care of works on paper, canvas and other media: storage, lighting, environmental conditions and handling — so your collection remains in peak condition.

  • Framing — Source custom-made framing to enhance the beauty of the artwork, reviewing different materials for all sizes and types of work.
  • Shipment & Installation — Best-market shipment service door to door, with full installation so the client has nothing to worry about.
  • Exhibition Design — Larger-scale installation: develop advanced plans, timelines, concepts and exhibition price lists to assist organisations.

02

Art Consultancy, Creative
Direction & Curation

We work with private collectors, developers, hospitality brands and organisations to build coherent, meaningful art programmes — whether acquiring a first work or shaping an entire collection strategy.

Our creative direction brings a strong curatorial perspective to spaces and projects: developing narratives, identifying artists and creating environments where art and context resonate together.

From artist commissions and exhibition development to brand partnerships, we bring taste, rigour and practical experience to every engagement.

  • Art Advice — Visit the property to advise on what type of art would fit and stand out in each area. Build a relationship to understand aspirations, taste and what the client is looking for.
  • Art Curation — Provide an array of ideas on how the art could be displayed, making the most of the space and its characteristics.
  • Negotiating & Commissioning — Work with artists to create the perfect piece for the project — negotiating price, timeline and deliverables.
