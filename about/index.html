Who we are

About Studio Kaku

Studio Kaku was founded on the belief that art deserves both the right frame and the right context.

We are a collaboration between two specialists — one rooted in the material and physical world of framing, installation and conservation; the other in the intellectual and cultural work of consultancy, direction and curation. Together, we offer a complete picture.

Our practice is built on close relationships: with artists, framers, collectors, institutions and the works themselves. We take time to understand what each project needs, and we bring craft and care to every stage of the process.

The Team

Yuki Kakuno

Framing, Installation & Art Care

Born in Tsushima and educated in New York, Yuki has been based in Tokyo for over 10 years, specializing in high-quality framing for artists and galleries. His work includes projects with the Tokyo American Club and the flagship Ginza gallery of Ceysson & Bénétière. Guided by the belief in "framing that lets the artwork speak," he continues to bring out the full charm of each piece.

jukaframing.com

Eleanor Ford

Art Consultancy, Creative Direction & Curation

Born in France and raised between the UK and Hong Kong, Eleanor studied Spatial Design at the University of the Arts London before moving to Japan. She has spent the past eight years creating art projects that span government installations, private residences, galleries, and international art fairs — most recently leading large-scale work at Haneda Airport.
